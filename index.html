Sleep Token

An enigmatic journey into music, emotion, and devotion.

Here's a time line of Sleep Token:

    2016

  • Sleep Token is founded, led by the enigmatic and masked figure known as Vessel. Their identity remains anonymous, adding to the band's mystery and lore.
  • The band releases their debut single, "Thread the Needle," introducing their signature blend of progressive metal, alternative rock, and ambient sounds.

    • 2017

  • Sleep Token releases their debut EP, one. Songs like "Nazareth" and "Fields of Elation" showcase their ethereal and haunting musical style.
  • The band begins cultivating a dedicated fanbase, drawn to their atmospheric sound and enigmatic identity.

    • 2018

  • Their second EP, Two, is released, featuring standout tracks like "Calcutta" and "Jericho." This EP further establishes Sleep Token’s sound and thematic elements.
  • Fans begin to speculate on the deeper lore surrounding Sleep Token, Vessel, and the worship of the deity known as Sleep.

    • 2019

  • Sleep Token releases their first full-length album, Sundowning
  • The album rollout is unique: tracks like "The Night Does Not Belong to God," "Sugar," and "Higher" are released one by one, every two weeks, at sundown.
  • Sundowning gains critical acclaim for its deeply emotional lyrics and cinematic soundscapes.

    • 2021

  • Second Album – This Place Will Become Your Tomb: Sleep Token releases their highly anticipated second album, featuring singles like "Alkaline," "The Love You Want," and "Fall for Me."
  • The album dives deeper into themes of love, loss, and worship, solidifying their position in the progressive metal and alternative scene.

    • 2022

  • Sleep Token’s music gains wider recognition thanks to their unique sound and growing presence in the festival circuit.
  • The band continues to maintain their mysterious persona, refusing interviews and focusing on their live performances.

    • 2023

  • Third Album – Take Me Back to Eden: Sleep Token releases their third studio album, which is hailed as a masterpiece by fans and critics.
  • Singles like "The Summoning" and "Chokehold" go viral, with "The Summoning" gaining widespread attention on platforms like TikTok.
  • Take Me Back to Eden marks the final chapter of a trilogy, tying together themes of devotion, sacrifice, and the enigmatic deity Sleep.

    • 2024

  • Sleep Token continues to tour globally, headlining festivals and performing to sold-out crowds. Their live shows are celebrated for their theatrical and ritualistic atmosphere, further cementing their legacy as one of the most unique acts in modern music.
